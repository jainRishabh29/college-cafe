Cafeteria


The kiet College Cafeteria is proud to be a partner in the success of quality education and the future opportunities of both present students and past alumni.


We offer a vast array of quality food items including fresh, hot quality north-indian, south-indian and chinese food selection, complete hot and cold sandwich selections and, of course, desserts and beverages at the best price.


So whether you are a student, faculty or staff member or a part of the local community wanting to dine in a fun, relaxed and affordable atmosphere, we encourage you to be our guest.
